What Makes a C&C Venue

Spaces that
photograph themselves.

i.

Character & light

Mansions, estates, studios, lofts, rooftops, gardens. Strong natural light, texture, and architectural detail that reads rich on camera.

ii.

Room to move

Multiple shooting zones so 30 creatives can spread out — indoor and outdoor, a few styled corners, space for a beauty station.

iii.

A real arrangement

Paid rental, trade, or content collaboration — your space, photographed by our room and shared with our community. We'll find what works.
