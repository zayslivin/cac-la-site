About

The story behind
the room.

Why Create & Connect exists, and what we're protecting.

The Founder

Nathalie Guerrero.

I started Create & Connect when I got pregnant and realized I needed a stream of income that could eventually become a real business — and give me freedom.

I'd been attending photo meets across LA and always left feeling like something was missing. *If they had just changed this one thing,* I'd think, *this could've been so much more productive, beautiful, and fun.*

So I built my own — the way I felt would actually benefit everyone in the room.

The Turning Point

When a side project
became a real one.

I realized this wasn't just another photo meet when people I respected started wanting to be part of it.

From a friend trusting me with their mansion to shoot in, to creatives who usually paid $30–$40 for photo meets willingly paying $95+ for a Create & Connect event — I knew people were seeing this as an experience, not a transaction.

That's when I committed: build it like it deserves to exist.

What We Protect

Quality over quantity.
Every time.

i.

Curated rooms.

Every attendee is intentional. Photographers selected for vision, models for talent, brands for taste. Not a marketplace — a room.

ii.

Elevated work.

Editorial-grade venues, considered styling, real creative direction. What you leave with is what you can show to agencies and brands.

iii.

Real collaboration.

Not a transaction. A room where photographers, models, designers, and brands actually build relationships that last past the event.

What This Isn't

Not a meetup. Not a marketplace.
Not a content mill.

Create & Connect is rooted in art, creativity, collaboration, and meaningful work. It can grow — but it will never lose the feeling that made people want to be part of it in the first place.
