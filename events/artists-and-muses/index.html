The Concept

Old-world elegance,
golden hills.

This room steps into old-world elegance — a white-columned Colonial manor on twelve acres of gardens, 2,200 feet up in the Gavilan Hills, where the air is cool and the mountains frame every horizon.

The Creative Collective leans into the oldest relationship in art: the one between the maker and the muse. This is not a meetup. It's a curated room of photographers, models, and a handful of brands and creative directors — chosen for taste, intention, and the kind of work that belongs in a portfolio.

Across six hours, you'll move through garden lawns, grand mansion facades, and light-filled interior halls — every corner built for editorial that feels timeless, painterly, and worth showing. Bring your best concepts. Leave with images you'll use for years.

What to Expect

Timeless work

Before You RSVP

Questions, answered.

What does it cost?

Photographers/creators: $100 early bird (first 50), then $150. Models: $50 early bird (first 25), then $100. Fifty model spots are also given out free through the in-person casting on July 26.

How does the free model casting work?

It's an in-person casting at Thatcher Manor on July 26 (time to be announced). Apply through the casting page; fifty models will be selected to attend The Creative Collective complimentary.

What's included?

Six hours at the estate, access to every photographer and model in the room, all designated shooting areas across the grounds and interiors, and the pre-event group chat with concepts, styling direction, and the attendee list.

What should I bring?

Come ready based on the free field guide on our site. Photographers: your kit and a styled concept or two. Models: looks that suit an old-world, editorial estate — we'll share styling direction in the group chat.

Where is it?

Thatcher Manor at Gavilan Hills — 22220 Gavilan Road, Lake Matthews, CA 92570. Parking and arrival details go out to confirmed attendees 48 hours before.

What's the refund policy?

Full refund up to 7 days before the event. After that, tickets are non-refundable but transferable — email hello@createconnectla.com to pass yours to another creative.
