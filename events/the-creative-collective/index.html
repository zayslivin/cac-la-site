The Creative Collective

An event made for creatives by creatives, at Thatcher Manor

Sunday, October 25  ·  1–7 PM

Thatcher Manor  ·  Lake Matthews, CA

Red Carpet Arrival  ·  Curated Networking  ·  Brand Activations

RSVP →
The Vibe

Where LA's creative
world meets.

Hollywood energy. Estate-level elegance. An event made for creatives by creatives. The Creative Collective brings photographers, models, brands, vendors, designers, and visionaries together for one elevated afternoon at a Colonial estate that comfortably accommodates 250+ guests in the Gavilan Hills.

This isn't a photoshoot at a pretty venue — it's a high-status creative social. Walk the red carpet, move through brand activations, and connect with the people actually building LA's creative scene.

Arrive dressed with intention. Network with brands, creatives, and collaborators. Leave with connections, content, and visibility.

The Experience

Six hours.
Built to be seen.

01

Red Carpet Arrival

A step-and-repeat and photographer coverage at the entrance. Make an entrance, get the arrival moment, and set the tone for the afternoon.

02

Curated Networking

Intentional introductions across photographers, models, designers, founders, and brands. A guest list built for real connections — not small talk.

03

Brand Activations

Sponsor and brand moments throughout the estate — beauty, fashion, lifestyle and more — with product placement and content opportunities.

04

Vendor Experience

A curated set of vendors — food, drinks, beauty, fashion, and lifestyle — so the day feels like an event, not an errand.

05

Content Sets

Styled sets and creative installations across the grounds for the photographers and models on the guest list. The content is a feature — not the whole story.

06

Be Seen

An elevated, dress-to-impress afternoon worth showing up for — and worth being seen at — with LA's creative industry in one place.

Before You RSVP

Questions, answered.

What is The Creative Collective?

A high-end creative networking event — red carpet arrival, brand activations, curated networking, vendors, and content sets — bringing LA's creative industry together for an elevated afternoon at Thatcher Manor.

What does it cost?

Creatives (photographers, content creators, designers): $100 early bird (first 50), then $150. Models: $50 early bird (first 25), then $100 — or get cast in free at the July 26 casting. Brands, sponsors, and vendors partner by inquiry.

How does the free model casting work?

It's an in-person casting at Thatcher Manor on July 26 (time to be announced). Apply through the casting page; fifty models will be selected to attend The Creative Collective complimentary.

Can my brand sponsor, activate, or vend?

Yes — that's a core part of the experience. Brands, sponsors, and vendors can host booths, activations, product placement, and content moments. Inquire to partner and we'll send options.

What's the dress code?

Dress to be seen — elevated and red-carpet ready. This is an afternoon worth showing up for, with photographer coverage throughout.

Where is it?

Thatcher Manor in the Gavilan Hills — a Colonial estate that comfortably accommodates 250+ guests. The exact address, parking, and arrival timing go to confirmed guests 48 hours before.

What's the refund policy?

Full refund up to 7 days before the event. After that, tickets are non-refundable but transferable — email hello@createconnectla.com to pass yours to another creative.
