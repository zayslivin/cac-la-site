The Concept

An editorial
worth keeping.

Our next room heads to Orange County — a styled, light-filled setting built for editorial work, chosen for texture, natural light, and the kind of detail that reads rich on camera.

This is not a meetup. It's a curated room of 10–15 photographers, 10–15 models, and a handful of designers, brands, and creative directors — chosen for taste, intention, and the kind of energy that produces magazine-grade work in three hours.

Expect dramatic lighting, soft jazz, a closed set, and zero distractions. Bring your best concepts. Leave with images that belong in your strongest portfolio.

Before You RSVP

Questions, answered.

What's included in the $90?

3 hours at a curated venue, access to all photographers/models in the room, light snacks and beverages, on-site beauty touch-up team, and the pre-event group chat with the full attendee list, concepts, and styling notes.

What should I bring?

Photographers: your camera, lighting if you prefer your own, a styled idea or two. Models: 2–3 looks (we'll share styling direction in advance), comfortable shoes for between shots, and your portfolio reference. Brands: product, lookbook, anything you want photographed.

What's the dress code?

Editorial-ready. We share a moodboard with the full theme in the group chat a week prior.

Where's the exact location?

Released to confirmed attendees 48 hours before the event. It's a private venue in Orange County — parking instructions, gate code, and arrival timing come with the address.

Can I get a refund?

Full refunds up to 7 days before the event. Within 7 days, we can transfer your spot to a future event but cannot refund — the venue is already booked against attendee count.

Is there parking?

Street parking available, and we'll share specifics in the day-of email. A small number of guest spots on the property are reserved for talent arriving with equipment.

How do I get final images?

Photographers retain their files. Most share previews within 48 hours and edited galleries within 2 weeks via the event group chat. Models can DM photographers directly to license/use specific frames.
